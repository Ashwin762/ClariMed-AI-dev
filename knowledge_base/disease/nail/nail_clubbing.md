# Nail Clubbing

## Overview

Nail clubbing is a condition in which the fingertips enlarge and the
nails curve downward around the fingertips. It usually develops
gradually and may be associated with underlying lung, heart, liver, or
gastrointestinal diseases rather than being a disease itself.

**Note:** This condition often has visible nail changes, and image
analysis may assist the AI. However, medical evaluation is necessary to
identify the underlying cause.

---

# Common Symptoms

- Curved or rounded nails

- Enlarged fingertips

- Softening of the nail bed

- Increased angle between the nail and cuticle

- Thickened fingertips

---

# Causes

Nail clubbing usually develops due to chronic medical conditions that
reduce oxygen levels or affect circulation.

### Common causes

Chronic lung disease, congenital heart disease, inflammatory bowel
disease, liver disease, lung cancer, and other chronic illnesses.

---

# Risk Factors

- Chronic lung disease

- Congenital heart disease

- Liver disease

- Family history

- Smoking

- Long-standing medical conditions

---

# Severity Classification

## 🟢 Low

- Mild nail curvature

- No associated symptoms

Recommendation: Schedule a routine medical evaluation.

---

## 🟡 Moderate

- Progressive enlargement of fingertips

- Nail changes with chronic cough or breathlessness

Recommendation: Consult a dermatologist and appropriate specialist for
further evaluation.

---

## 🔴 High

Any of the following:

- Clubbing associated with severe breathlessness

- Chest pain

- Persistent coughing with blood

- Rapid worsening of symptoms

Recommendation: Seek immediate medical evaluation to identify the
underlying cause.

---

# Home Care

- Monitor nail changes.

- Avoid smoking.

- Maintain a healthy lifestyle.

- Follow medical advice for any underlying illness.

---

# Prevention

- Avoid smoking.

- Manage chronic medical conditions.

- Attend regular health check-ups.

- Seek early treatment for lung and heart diseases.

---

# When to Consult a Doctor

Consult a doctor if:

- Fingertips become enlarged.

- Nails become increasingly curved.

- Clubbing develops without a known cause.

- You experience cough or breathing difficulty.
