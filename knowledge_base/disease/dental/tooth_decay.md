# Tooth Decay

## Overview

Tooth decay occurs when acids produced by bacteria in dental plaque gradually erode tooth enamel, forming cavities. It's one of the most common chronic conditions worldwide and is largely preventable with good oral hygiene.

---

# Common Symptoms

- Toothache or spontaneous pain
- Sensitivity to hot, cold, or sweet foods
- Visible dark spots, pits, or holes in the tooth
- Pain when biting or chewing
- Bad breath

---

# Causes

### Bacterial acid production

Plaque bacteria metabolize sugars into acids that dissolve enamel over time.

### Contributing factors

Frequent sugary snacks, poor brushing habits, dry mouth, and inadequate fluoride exposure.

---

# Risk Factors

- Frequent consumption of sugary foods or drinks
- Inadequate brushing and flossing
- Dry mouth (reduced saliva)
- Lack of fluoride exposure
- Deep grooves in molars

---

---

# Severity Classification

## 🟢 Low

- Early white/brown spots
- No pain

Recommendation: Improve oral hygiene, schedule a routine dental check-up.

---

## 🟡 Moderate

- Visible cavity
- Sensitivity or intermittent pain

Recommendation: See a dentist — a filling is likely needed.

---

## 🔴 High

Any of the following:

- Severe, persistent toothache
- Facial swelling
- Fever with tooth pain

Recommendation: Seek prompt dental care — may indicate an abscess.

---

# Home Care

- Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste.
- Floss daily.
- Reduce sugary snacks and drinks.
- Rinse with water after meals.

---

# Prevention

- Maintain consistent brushing and flossing.
- Use fluoride toothpaste and consider fluoride treatments.
- Limit sugary and acidic foods.
- Get regular dental cleanings and check-ups.

---

# When to Consult a Doctor

Consult a dentist if:

- You notice a visible cavity, dark spot, or hole
- Sensitivity or pain persists
- It's been over six months since your last check-up

---

# Emergency Warning Signs

Seek prompt dental or medical care if:

- Severe throbbing pain
- Facial or jaw swelling
- Fever accompanying tooth pain
- Difficulty swallowing or breathing
