# Leukoplakia

## Overview

Leukoplakia is a condition characterized by thick, white patches that
develop inside the mouth, including the tongue, cheeks, and gums. These
patches cannot be easily scraped off and may develop due to chronic
irritation. Although many cases are harmless, some may become
precancerous and require further evaluation.

**Note:** Visible white patches may assist image analysis, but
diagnosis requires examination by a dental professional.

---

# Common Symptoms

- White patches inside the mouth

- Thickened areas on the tongue or cheeks

- Patches that cannot be wiped away

- Mild discomfort while eating

- Rough oral patches

---

# Causes

Leukoplakia usually develops due to long-term irritation of the oral
tissues.

### Common causes

Smoking, chewing tobacco, chronic irritation from rough teeth or
dentures, alcohol use, and poor oral hygiene.

---

# Risk Factors

- Tobacco use

- Alcohol consumption

- Poor oral hygiene

- Ill-fitting dentures

- Chronic mouth irritation

- Older age

---

# Severity Classification

## 🟢 Low

- Small painless white patch

- No difficulty eating

Recommendation: Schedule a dental evaluation.

---

## 🟡 Moderate

- Enlarging white patches

- Mild discomfort while eating

Recommendation: Consult a dentist for examination.

---

## 🔴 High

Any of the following:

- Rapidly enlarging patch

- Patch with ulceration or bleeding

- Persistent pain

- Difficulty swallowing

Recommendation: Seek prompt dental evaluation to rule out oral cancer.

---

# Home Care

- Maintain good oral hygiene.

- Avoid smoking and tobacco.

- Limit alcohol consumption.

- Eat a balanced diet.

- Attend regular dental check-ups.

---

# Prevention

- Avoid tobacco products.

- Limit alcohol use.

- Maintain proper oral hygiene.

- Correct poorly fitting dentures.

- Schedule routine dental examinations.

---

# When to Consult a Doctor

Consult a dentist if:

- White patches persist for more than two weeks.

- The patch enlarges.

- The lesion becomes painful or starts bleeding.

- You have difficulty swallowing.
